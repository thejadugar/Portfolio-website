"Ex-Planet"

Explanet is a survival game where players explore a monster-filled planet to collect three antennas and escape. You can build, upgrade, and repair weapons and materials to survive attacks from small and giant monsters. Strategic exploration and resource management are key as you fight to stay alive. The game ends when the antennas are secured, and the signal is transmitted from the computer.

"Kisan A Day"

Kisan A Day is a casual farming simulation game where players manage their own farm, grow crops, raise animals, and produce goods. The game offers a relaxing and engaging experience with intuitive controls. Players can trade their produce with neighbors, customize their farm. It's all about building and managing a thriving farm and enjoying the simple joys of country life.

"HORRID-HORDE"

Amidst a foreboding castle, players confront a diverse array of zombies, each with unique abilities and weaknesses. Navigating through dimly lit corridors, they scavenge for resources and uncover hidden secrets to outmaneuver the relentless undead horde. Survival demands strategic thinking and quick reflexes as players fight their way to freedom through the labyrinthine fortress of fear.

"Chameleon Color Rush"

Chameleon Color Rush is an endless runner game where a chameleon hops across platforms of varying colors. Players must swipe chameleon's color with the platform, ensuring it lands safely. A mismatch results in the chameleon getting hilariously smacked by a racket, ending the run. The gameplay grows faster, testing players' reflexes and focus as they aim for high scores With vibrant visuals and dynamic mechanics, it offers an exciting challenge.

"Blitz Endless waves"

Dive into the depths of Aqua Rush, an adrenaline-fueled 3D endless runner game set in a mesmerizing underwater world. Players surf atop waves in an endless ocean teeming with life, navigating through dynamic obstacles such as coral reefs, underwater caverns, and treacherous whirlpools. However, the real challenge lies in facing off against a diverse array of water creatures acting as enemies.

"Zungle Town"

Welcome to "ZungleTown," My debut creation as a game developer! In this low-graphic adventure, players explore a jungle and a deserted town infested with zombies. With simple yet engaging graphics, immerse yourself in the eerie atmosphere of survival. Encounter basic zombies with tattered clothes and hungry eyes as you scavenge for resources and weapons to fend off the undead.
